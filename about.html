About the newsletter

You're not underskilled.
You're underleveraged.

Most designers with 10+ years of experience are overqualified for the work they're doing and underequipped for the business they could be building.

You got good. Really good. Then you kept getting better — at the thing that keeps you employed, not the thing that builds something for you.

There's a version of your career where your expertise compounds. Where you stop trading hours for outcomes. Where the last decade starts working for you instead of the other way around.

Most designers never find that version. Not because they lack skill. Because nobody taught them that game existed.

That's what this newsletter is about.

One issue, every Friday. Under five minutes.

One idea at the intersection of craft, strategy, and leverage — the kind of thing a sharp colleague would share over coffee, not a LinkedIn post.

A ready-to-use AI prompt tied to that week's topic
Three resources worth your time
One clear next step
No fluff. No "in today's fast-paced world."

"Designers, PMs, and tech leaders who've spent years building leverage for everyone else — and are starting to wonder when they get a turn."

20+ years in design. Six in architecture, fourteen in tech. I'm not writing from the summit. I'm one step ahead on the path, writing it down as I go.

That's the only kind of advice worth taking.
